What is ETWS?

The Earthquake and Tsunami Warning System (ETWS) is designed to deliver timely alerts for imminent natural disasters, such as earthquakes and tsunamis, to mobile users in affected areas.

Why is ETWS Important?

ETWS provides critical warnings to mitigate the impact of disasters on human life. It enables governments to send out emergency alerts via mobile networks, helping to save lives and prevent injuries.

Example Message

        Message ID: 1234
        Warning Type: Earthquake
        Alert Level: High
        Repetition Period: 5s
